Hackers for the Advancement of Civilization and Knowledge
Join Us

H.A.C.K. is a membership organization driven by individuals who are dedicated to advancing humanity through innovation and collaboration. Our purpose is to cultivate effective solutions for the many problems our world is facing. We do this by pooling together people from all walks of life and empowering them with the tools and resources needed to actualize H.A.C.K. initiatives.

It's a hackerspace fueled by open discussion and relentless innovation. A decentralized think-tank that serves to encourage its members to stay proactive and utilize new strategies to address problems in their communities.

Members recognize the problems in their communities and strive to apply their strengths to address those those issues. Where our collective action is our greatest power, together we can develop effective solutions for the many problems our world is facing.
